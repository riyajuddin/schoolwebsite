ABOUT US


We are the next-gen, highly secured communication tool for school-parent conversations designed to promote high levels of engagement (like never before) leading to enthusiastic collaborations between the parent community and the school community resulting in improved performance of the student/child.

In today’s world of smart-phones (smart communication), the old ways of ‘one-way’ unreliable communication, letters, printed notices etc. etc. are almost obsolete.

That’s where skoolsync comes in... the most definitive and simple smart communication tool for the most progressive and modern schools as well as for the most forward-looking and caring parents.

skoolsync instantly connects schools and parents on 1 single platform enabling quick conversations leading to incredible engagement!! Skoolsync is the faster and more reliable messaging option for a safe and true 2-way communication on smart-phones!

We, at skoolsync, invite schools and parents alike to experience the POWER of staying connected and staying updated anywhere, anytime!

FEATURES


  • Two (2) Way Messaging

    Instant Interactions | Private Chats | Group Chats
    Chat real-time with Class Teachers, Class/School Groups and Parents.

  • Shared Media

    Multimedia Gallery | Student Portfolios
    Share class-room/school activities with Parents via photos, videos & audio.

  • Transport Related Alerts

    Trip Status Notification | Exception Alerts
    Alerts to Parents on ‘Exception Basis’: Bus breakdown, Traffic jams etc.

  • Leave Request

    Online Request | Easy Application & Approval
    Apply digitally and get timely approvals. Track online and view archive.

  • Fee Payment

    Fee Payment Status Notification | Timely Alerts
    Timely reminders to Parents and pay fees online via School’s own payment gateway

  • Timetable & Calendar

    Digital Timetable & Calendar | Repository of Events, Holidays
    Get Class Time-tables, Events Info, Holidays List on your smart-phones

  • Attendance Alerts

    Attendance Notification | Exception Alerts
    Notify Parents immediately if Student found absent, Record absence

  • Exam Results Alerts

    Exam Results Notification | Timely Alerts
    Timely notify Parents as and when the exam results are declared
